An Educational, Fun, and Socializing Game


You upload a picture (ANY PICTURE) and find out the name, location, and story behind the image you have loaded. Or go ahead and press the "Around the World" button to learn about a random image that will be provided for you. You will be timed a total of 10 seconds to guess your answer. Good luck and have FUN learning!
